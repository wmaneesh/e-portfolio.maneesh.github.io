I will be going into my last year of Computer Engineering at York University. The reason I love this program is because it’s a combination of both electrical engineering and software engineering. I have always been passionate about learning robotics and I realized that Computer Engineering is the stream that has most integration with robotics and will allow me to gain the necessary knowledge to achieve my future goals.

For the past two years I have been fortunate enough to land two co-op positions. One at CIBC as an IT-Project Coordinate and the other at 4PAY as a Technical Analyst. These two positions allowed me to gain valuable industry experience which will help me immensely in my future career. The working environment was quite different between CIBC and 4PAY because CIBC was a rather large financial corporation, while 4PAY a small fintrec company. This played a big role in the amount of experience I gained and the learning I did. 4PAY had a higher rewarding experience for me because of the small team structure they had. This let do a great closely-knit community. Unlike my placement at CIBC, I was able to engage with my co-workers and learn from them a lot more. CIBC, being a large financial institution, had its downside of having a complex working environment.

My future career goal would be to take my skills I learned as a Project Coordinator and a Technical Analyst and apply it secure a position as a Project Manager for a software or embedded development company. The experience I’ve gained during my co-op placement is invaluable as it directly correlates to my career goals in the future.
